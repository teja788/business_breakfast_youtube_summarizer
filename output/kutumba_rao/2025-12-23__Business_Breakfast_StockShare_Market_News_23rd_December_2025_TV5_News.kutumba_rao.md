## Market view
- **Bullish; the rally should continue** — he expects only very minor dips from here. He spoke very bullishly the previous morning and reaffirms a **reversal trend** is in place.
- The signal is **market-breadth improvement after several days, backed by both price action and volume action** — even weaker stocks are rising alongside stronger ones, which shows changing confidence in the undercurrent.
- Lesson on **"sell on rallies"**: don't panic-sell good stocks at lower levels; sell only at ideal valuations. Examples of sharp two-session bounces: **Jupiter Wagons**, **Idea Forge**. A relieving session for retail investors (yesterday and last Friday).
- **Macro positives**: Nov core industrial growth +1.8% (decent vs weak Oct); **FII selling essentially stopped** (only Rs 457 cr sold); rupee down just 1 paisa to 89.68. **Gold & silver both strong** — partly geopolitics (US seizing Venezuela crude tankers) driving crude and gold.
- **VIX below 10** (no fear, some recovery/confidence). Nifty in **striking distance** of the lifetime high; a single close above **26,200** should lead to a new lifetime high (level **26,326**). Once above 26,326 it is **uncharted territory** — harder to predict.
- **Options/OI**: call activity building at **26,500** (monthly); huge OI at **26,200**; puts shifted to **26,000** (major support). PCR jumped to **1.42**; market moving into a **safe zone**.
- **Derivatives colour**: speculative longs in Infosys, Muthoot Finance, IRCTC, BDL; speculative shorts in Cholamandalam Finance, Nuvama, Astral, Dalmia Bharat; delivery buying in Britannia, Kotak Bank, Delhivery, Hero Motor, Bajaj Fin. Ban list: only **Sammaan Capital** (not a concern).
- **Fund flows**: NFO collections roughly halved in 2025 (~Rs 63,000 cr to Nov vs ~Rs 1.8 lakh cr in 2024, back to 2023 levels), but SIP flows keep supporting confidence. **India seen turning back into favour** vs AI-driven markets (US/Taiwan/Korea) as a non-AI diversification play.
- **Bank Nifty needs a turnaround** — no large gains yesterday (PNB +1.5%; SBI, Kotak weakish). Watch whether IT/finance exuberance continues.
- **Strategy**: exit weak, no-earnings-visibility stocks (esp. Q3) into rallies as a selling opportunity; **buy strong stocks that have fallen recently (contrarian)**. Watch F&O exits like **IRCTC, NCC, HFCL** for covering bounces; news-driven names **Prestige, GPT Infra, Antony Waste**. **Stay invested** with a distant trailing stop-loss.
- **Cement consolidation** (Ambuja/ACC/Orient) is turning the sector into a **4-5 player game** (Aditya Birla/UltraTech, Adani, Dalmia Bharat, Shree Cement, and Ramco in the South) — future good when controlled by leaders. Confirmed swap ratios later with Ramakrishna: **328 Ambuja per 100 ACC** (Rs 10 paid-up ACC vs Rs 2 paid-up), **33 Ambuja per 100 Orient**.
