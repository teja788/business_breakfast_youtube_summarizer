## Market view
- The markets have **nearly slipped into a weak trend**. The Nifty repeatedly attempted but **failed to cross 26,000**, and slipped/corrected from there.
- **IT was the core drag** — the top six F&O/Nifty losers were all IT (Tech Mahindra, Infosys, TCS, HCL Tech, Wipro), with nearly every stock down ~5%+.
- Root cause is **AI-disruption fear from the US**: **Anthropic's** new version launch plus reported **~$30bn funding at a ~$380bn valuation**. The market can't gauge how far AI disruption hits the software sector, so until this **AI panic subsides there will be no significant recovery**. Even on good results expect higher-level distribution, not buying.
- Weak institutional support: **FIIs only Rs 108 cr, DIIs only Rs 277 cr**; rupee ~**90.61**. The **yen** had its best week vs the dollar in ~15 months (Takaichi's election win → fund flows to Japan), so until India attracts funds, gains will be limited.
- Cautious on **Indo-US deal fine print** and **Bangladesh elections** (prefers BNP over Jamaat-e-Islami).
- Doomsayer scenario noted (TCS cap → Rs 5 lakh cr, Infosys → Rs 3 lakh cr) but **not an immediate fear** — still, companies that don't reinvent face that risk. "AI disruption is one disruption which cannot be ignored."
