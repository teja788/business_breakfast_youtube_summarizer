- **Korea**: Kospi hit a downward circuit breaker last week; SK Hynix + Samsung are ~60% of that exchange's market cap, so the drag persists. Would not compare that to India.

## Question to the SETL data-center guests
Kutumba Rao told the SETL/GScale promoters he reads them as **entering a competitive, non-unique field** — cooling equipment and power products already have large players (he named **Kirloskar Chiller** as a data-center cooling supplier) and a dozen-plus companies. He asked whether **margins can be sustained** against emerging competition and what **unique IP / USP** gives them an edge. (Management replied on their RPP/PDC and liquid-cooling CDU IP, integration + turnkey model, and 4-6 month timeline savings.) He gave **no buy/sell call** on SETL.
