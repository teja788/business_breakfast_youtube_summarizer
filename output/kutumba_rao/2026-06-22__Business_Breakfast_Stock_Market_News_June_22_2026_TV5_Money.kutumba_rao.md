## Market view
- **Consolidation mode** expected; **24,000** should hold as a floor. Strong buying from lower levels, especially in the **broader market** — where Nifty/Bank Nifty rose ~1.5% last week, Midcap/Smallcap rose ~3-4%. Watching whether the market is becoming complacent and slowing at higher levels.
- **Domestic supply**: both the **NSE IPO** and **Jio Platforms IPO** will lock up money and have some impact on secondary markets.
- **Global cues softening**: expects the US-Iran situation to de-escalate eventually (deal "unavoidable for the Americans"); Strait of Hormuz to reopen; crude likely drifting toward the **$70s**, which would relieve India. The world is used to **$80-90** crude; a fall is positive, but a rise won't bite hard until **$100**.
- **Dollar** likely dominant for a couple of months (index near ~11-month high post-Fed) until US mid-term clarity.
- **FII flows** turning: net FII buying ~**Rs 3,386 crore** last week; even marginal stable buying is positive. **Rupee** had touched 96.96; a move below **94** (~94.34) would be more positive; FCNR liberalization could aid debt inflows.
- **Monsoon is the biggest uncertainty**: ~**30-40% deficit**; distribution matters for agri output, rural demand, inflation. If it clarifies positively, markets could strengthen.
