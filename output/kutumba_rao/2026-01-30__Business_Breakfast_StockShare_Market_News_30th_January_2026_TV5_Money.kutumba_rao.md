## Market view
- **Volatility certain** ahead of the Budget — pre-Budget nervousness plus global cues (on/off signals that the **US may attack Iran**). Today's weakness is **nothing to be much afraid of**.
- Bullish structurally: "**Make India Indispensable**." Calls for a **big-bang budget** (like Manmohan Singh's radical budget) to withstand global uncertainties and put India "into a different orbit."
- Cites **policy dynamism + purposeful governance**: GST 2.0, income-tax relief, new labour codes (one-time provisions like **ITC's Rs 273 cr**, Infosys earlier — stability after), easier green-cover/environmental clearances, Atmanirbhar Bharat manufacturing push, good monsoons, low external liabilities.
- **Economic Survey** (liked it): trade position flipped from ~**$23-24bn surplus to ~$6-7bn deficit** (geopolitics + tariffs); needs **export diversification** without protectionist barriers; **private investment lagging** (CEA flagged). Also notably addressed crypto/**stablecoins** (US GENIUS Act, Bitcoin >$1 lakh; **Tether bought ~140 tonnes gold** over 14-15 months — a driver of the gold rally).
- **Bottom line: buy-on-dips market, NOT a sell-on-rallies market.** At low levels, despite risk, **buying is the best strategy as of now.**

## Asset-allocation message
- Don't chase one hot asset class (gold/silver/Gold BeES/Silver BeES). Gold's extra ~25% rise here is partly **rupee depreciation** — will correct when rupee appreciates.
- Foreign-equity FOMO: South Korea +80%, US/China/Japan/Europe +20-40%, Nifty only +5% over 1 yr. But over 10 yrs India returned **8.4%** (dollar terms) vs China **4.4%**; Nifty since inception **11.75%** (dollar terms). **Equities will outperform.**
- Keep **min 50% in equities** (younger investors 75-80% fine). Don't neglect small caps — small/mid/micro/large each rise in their time.
