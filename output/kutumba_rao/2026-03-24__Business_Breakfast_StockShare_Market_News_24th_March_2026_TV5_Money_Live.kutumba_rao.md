## Market view

- **Don't give too much significance to the rally.** For it to sustain, the **geopolitical situation must improve significantly**. Trump has given only a five-day pause; events in between must be watched. If none occur, markets may slowly stabilize and recover — but **caution should be the watchword**.

Usually small-caps move after large-caps stabilize.
