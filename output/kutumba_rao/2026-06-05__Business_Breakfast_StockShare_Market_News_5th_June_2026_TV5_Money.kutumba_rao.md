## Market view
- **RBI policy = likely a non-event.** VRR auction: 75,000-cr notified but only ~17,000-cr subscribed → decent inter-bank liquidity; weighted avg rate 5.26% → no repo change likely. Policy will be commentary-driven, especially on rupee weakness vs the dollar (forex-reserve conservation). Echoes IMF's Gita Gopinath: RBI must balance inflation risk between higher oil and currency depreciation; likely on hold; building trust in government data matters more.
- **Doubts a major Nifty move today.** Nifty held above 23,300 all week. **FIIs in record shorts (~2.6-2.7 lakh contracts)** + heavy underperformance → any small positive trigger could spark a **500-600 pt Nifty rally** and a revisit of **24,000**; conditions favour taking a little long risk. A possible RBI + Centre "coordinated action" (more FII sops, MFN treaty) could be that trigger.
- **Mid/small-caps are where the action is.** Portfolios up 20-40% since early March; some SMEs +30-50%. Large-cap participation may begin soon (attractive valuations). Pick resilient outperformers with very bullish management commentary (cited Olectra +20% post-results, Laurus Labs, KRN Heat Exchangers +15-20%, Sai Parenterals 425→550).
- **Be nimble; focus long-term.** Geopolitics has become "boring"; concentrating on short-term moves invites mistakes. Stay invested in good long-term stocks; **exit weak small/mid-caps that got carried up in the rally and rotate into new quality stocks.** No rule to hold the "same old companies" — cycles change.
- **Watch points:** IPO supply (MNC IPOs — Coca-Cola bottler, Hyundai, LG; Walmart billion-$ talk) as a secondary-market drag; corporate-governance flare-ups (Rajesh Exports 400 GB rejoinder); Bitcoin multi-month low (~$61k, >50% off ATH) as a risk-aversion signal; monsoon spread/El Nino. Institutional-radar names: **Go Digit** (bulk deal ~50 lakh, +11% to new high; stay invested near 52-wk high), **Goldman buying "Million Brains/Garuda(?)"**, **CG Power** (switchgear facility commissioned, may be hyperactive), **HDFC AMC** (paused gold-ETF/FoF lumpsum). Momentum names rising again: Bagaria Industries, IRCTC/ITDC, Zen Technologies (+8%), JBM, **Adani Enterprises** (big morning volume — possible big Adani-group move).
