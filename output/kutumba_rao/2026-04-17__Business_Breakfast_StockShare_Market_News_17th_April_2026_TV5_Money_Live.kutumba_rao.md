## EV / data-centre / battery view
- **EV space — TVS Motors is the first choice** (captured two-wheeler market; better bet even in declines). Ola/Ather can be looked at; watch for a turnaround.
- **Data centres** — more bullish on **suppliers** than operators; fears the operator business becomes commoditised once many centres are commissioned (power/water needs squeeze margins). **Not buying any major data-centre stock now.** Suppliers on the radar, e.g. **MTAR** (makes parts for its US partner, feeding data centres indirectly).
- **Battery energy storage systems** — has doubts; players say power cost won't fall below ~350 paise, so limited consumer benefit; market won't grow "humongously" without cost-cutting → stay cautious.
