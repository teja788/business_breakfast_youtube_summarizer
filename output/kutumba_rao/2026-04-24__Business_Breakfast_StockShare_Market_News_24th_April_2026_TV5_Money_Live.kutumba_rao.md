## Market view
- **The positive open should not be trusted.** Yesterday the **24,200-300 support band** had to hold — Nifty bounced but **closed below 24,200**. Even if a relief rally opens today, it must **stay above 24,300** to sustain; as long as that doesn't happen, a **retest of 24,000** is a fairly definite possibility. **Keep 24,000 as the stop-loss** for Nifty positions, trail it, and protect profits.
- **All the action has shifted to the broader market.** Nobody is watching Nifty/Sensex any more, and the statistics show it: **F&O turnover down ~25% MoM (292 → 215 lakh cr)** while **cash turnover reached its highest level since July 2024** (roughly a two-year high). April returns: **Nifty/Sensex +8%** versus **mid-cap +13.2%** and **small-cap +17%**. Market patterns are changing and must be understood.
- **Options/sentiment**: heavy unwinding at **25,000 calls** yesterday; highest monthly **calls now at 24,500** — but a gap-up that fizzles will bring unwinding there too, with **settlement next week**. **Puts piled at 24,000**; if Nifty closes near 24,000 a die-hard could buy the **24,000 monthly put** (two days, weekend ahead). **PCR fell 1.18-1.2 → 1.02 → 0.92** at open — only below **0.9**, closer to **0.7**, take a positional trade. **VIX traded above 19 intraday, closed 18.6** — proof the bulls are losing confidence.
- **Rupee/crude/gold**: rupee falling a **fourth successive day**, crossed **94**; may retest the old low or head to **95** — doubts whether the RBI's steps have fizzled out; returning FII selling is a likely cause (**FIIs -Rs 3,250 cr**, **DIIs -Rs 940 cr** yesterday). The **dollar posted its first weekly gain in three weeks**. **Crude turning strong again is clearly a negative for India.** **Gold (24k) stuck at 1543** and not falling — fear has settled back into equities.
- **Stocks insulated from West Asia/crude can see unexpected rallies or dead-cat bounces.** Examples from yesterday: a beaten-down name at an all-time low **jumped 20%** merely on guiding to a marginal profit after full provisioning; innerwear/garment names **Rupa and Lovable +16-18%**; **IRM Energy +16%** on over 7 crore shares (flagged by him the previous morning); **Camlin Fine Sciences +15%** on ~3 crore shares (6x normal); **Piramal Pharma +7%** on 5.5 crore volume; **Zodiac Energy** newly on screen. - **F&O positioning**: speculative longs in **Policybazaar, Lupin, Jio Finance, UltraTech Cement** — in **Jio Finance** volumes developed heavily and **OI is now ~20 crore**, "the suspicion arises that something is cooking." Shorts built in **Mphasis, Force Motors, Hindustan Zinc, Motilal Oswal** — signs of further correction from current levels. Conviction delivery buying in **Britannia, Phoenix, Prestige, Cholamandalam Finance, Kotak Bank**; private banks looked resilient. Ban list: only **SAIL**.
