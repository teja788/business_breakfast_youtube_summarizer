## Stock calls (viewer Q&A)

### 52-week-high strategy — Radico, Sansera Engineering, Hitachi, Bans (caller Mohan, who asked for Kutumba Rao)
- Buying stocks as they make 52-week highs is **typically a good strategy**: a 52-week high (even in a bearish market) happens "with some reason" — operator-driven, results, or sector changes — so confirm the change is positive before buying.
- Method: **protect profit** with a **trailing stop-loss** placed at the last high (e.g. Radico near its 3-year high ~Rs 3,600 → trail a stop at ~3,250–3,350). This breakout method works best for **short/medium-term**, not the long term.
- For the **long term**, these are all good **wealth-creating** stocks — don't buy only at the highs; **buy on dips and do SIPs**. He specifically named **Hitachi, Sansera, Bans** as worth SIP-ing.
- Verdict: **Buy / Accumulate (SIP)** the named names (Hitachi, Sansera Engineering, Bans, Radico).

### Bondada Engineering (viewer Sravani, bought at Rs 435, ~25% loss)
- **Hold** — no need to book the loss. Huge order book (referenced an earlier interview with promoter Mr Raghvendra); solar EPC companies are picking up again. The viewer entered high (~Rs 435, ~Rs 100 above the current price), so it needs patience.

### Waaree Energy & CDSL (viewer Srinivas, wants 100% in 5 years)
- **Buy / Hold for long term** — that kind of return is plausible over five years. Capital markets keep generating volumes and new listings; **CDSL** has a steady (paid) business with no problem; **Waaree** is in the renewables space. "Five years, 100% returns is possible."
