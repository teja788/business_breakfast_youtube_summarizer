## Market view
- **Proactive government is the key positive.** The Rs 2.55 lakh crore stimulus (MSME/airline credit guarantee, akin to the Covid-era package), plus reforms (e.g. expedited 60-day clearance for rare-earths/PCBs and ~40 sub-sectors, including China proposals), should help maintain the country's growth rate and keep the economy stable. With no state elections for ~10 months, the govt can focus on the economy and corporate earnings could improve this quarter.
- **India is "most resilient among emerging markets"** for global shocks — held up during the pandemic, the 2022 Fed tightening cycle, and last year's tariff tensions. As long as governments stay proactive, those countries' stock markets stay fine (cites **Intel up ~13%**, a multibagger since the US govt stake).
- **PSUs**: were underperforming, now stabilizing; if the govt monitors further progress, PSU stocks have clear signs of leading the market.
