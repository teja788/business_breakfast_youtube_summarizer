## Market view
- Today will be **weak**, Vasanth garu. **24,000 is the key point** — as long as Nifty trades below it, markets stay weak. Yesterday closed near 23,800 with deep cuts.
- Recovery depends on the **geopolitical situation** and **US-Iran negotiation progress**. **Crude back near USD 100/bbl** is a negative, coupled with the **PM's austerity statements** which have stoked public fears of being "on the edge of an economic crisis."
- Expects the market to go **range-bound, locked in 23,600-24,500**. Intraday: if Nifty opens and trades below **23,800**, risk rises; holding above 23,800 by end of day gives some safety.
- Activity will be in the **broader market (mid/small caps)**, not frontline stocks. **Aggressive buying is not advisable** — prepare for stock-specific moves. Avoid stocks whose volumes appear one day and vanish the next; stay in stocks with **sustained 2-3 day volumes forming higher bottoms / higher tops**.
- Rupee has begun weakening again.
