## Market view
- Everyone is in a **sell-off mindset**; Nifty is back below the **26,000** mark. The ~150-point Nifty pop on the **RBI 25 bps cut** was treated as a **one-off** — the market's real fear is the **US Fed decision** and the **India-US trade deal**.
- Trade-deal will happen **"with many question marks."** Trump's rice-tariff talk (partly envy over the Indo-Russia/Putin friendship) hit rice shares; fresh **US Fed uncertainty** (macro data not fully supportive).
- Yesterday was the **biggest fall since 25 September**. **Market breadth is extremely weak** — BSE declines **3,460** vs advances **843** (~4:1), continuously weakening month-on-month since April, now weakest as December arrives. **Until breadth improves it is not safe; expecting the market to rise is foolishness.**
- **FII selling continuous**, no near-term buying signs while the **rupee stays weak, above 90**. Oil eased a bit — "nothing to boast of." Equities have struggled the whole calendar year (weak corporate earnings, trade tensions, trade-deal uncertainty, and now year-end/Christmas holiday mood among foreign fund managers).
- Unless the RBI cut translates into **better Q3 results**, the market keeps struggling; a small rise possible only after Q3.
- **BSE market cap down 7 lakh crore+** in one-two days — recovering that is not easy. **~527 small/midcaps at 52-week lows**; Nifty Midcap -~1,000 pts, Smallcap -~450 pts.
- Only **3 Nifty stocks green** (Tech Mahindra, Wipro, HCL Tech) — rupee weakness props their export earnings; may look better at Q3.
- Gains/Aeons-type stock -11% to 3,800 on governance doubts; Indigo -9%; PSU banks + a couple of defence names led the damage (**BDL -6%, RITCO -6%, Mazagon -5%**, PG, Canara Bank). In FnO only 5 stocks rose (adding MFSL, Policy Bazaar, +half-to-1%). Volume pockets: Matrimony (+~10%), Precot (~+14%), Latent View, Dredging Corp (+~16%).
- **IPO market drained a lot of money** — until IPO froth cools and value/buying returns to the secondary market, no quick rise.
