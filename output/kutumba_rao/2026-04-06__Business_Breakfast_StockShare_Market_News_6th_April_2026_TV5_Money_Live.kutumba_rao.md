- **Market is in a "sell-on-rise" mood**, with earnings season ahead. Stocks with weak earnings visibility may get hammered (war used as excuse); strong performers rewarded, but only in a "very limited way."
- **History after war shocks:** Iraq war −10%, took 37 days to recover (+13%); Russia–Ukraine −7%+, recovered ~10% in 10 days; Hamas–Israel −4–5%, took 33 days (+5%). This US–Israel–Iran war already corrected 10%+; expect **at most another ~10% recovery, and not swift** — so sector selection must be careful.

Exceptions: Mankind Pharma, Anand Rathi, ICICI Pru AMC, KFin. ICICI Pru AMC's OFS (foreign promoter + ICICI Bank) is understandable as it met minimum-shareholding norms.
