## Market view
- **Andhra Pradesh / data centres:** AP (Vizag) is becoming THE centre for data centres in India — "the cornerstone." Reliance is already in; Bharti Airtel also showing interest. CM hosted a dinner/discussions.
- **Global / macro:** Market still complex — geopolitics fragile (Iran; pressure on China/Russia). Unless a quick solution comes the issue could escalate; but next 3-4 days may bring interesting developments. Nasdaq/S&P near new highs are sustaining; wait-and-watch below. Hopes for a continuing ceasefire and Hormuz-opening clarity in 2-3 days.
- **Risk-on in mid/small caps** — clearly visible, "no second thoughts." Beaten-down names that corrected sharply over the last ~1.5 years are recovering on **huge volumes**: e.g. a consistently weak stock up **15%**, **Mahindra Financial Services** ~3 cr+ volume and **~+10%**, **Tamilnadu Mercantile Bank +8%**, a local speculative counter **+20%**. (Mahindra Holidays fell ~4% on weak results despite volume.) But warns this **may be a short-term trade** — watch West Bengal exit polls and the first-week-of-May results carefully.
- **Sun Pharma acquisition** was the major market mover yesterday — biggest Indian acquisition since Tata Steel/Corus; the two will run separately for a couple of years until full merger. Expects an impact on pharma stocks broadly and possible **takeover targets among small pharma**; also thinks similar **domestic** large-cap-buys-small-cap attempts could happen.
- **Results/themes:** Summer ("super El Niño," 45-50°C) favours beverages (e.g. **Varun Beverages** — good sales), AC/fan companies, packaged water, power-equipment, EMS — decent **Q1 FY27** expected. Broader FY27 may see geopolitical effects on some sectors.
- **IT** performed a bit better (Tech Mahindra, Infosys, etc.) but sustaining the rally is doubtful.
- **VIX fell** well → confidence improving.
