Kutumba Rao was present and named throughout (addressed as "Kutumba Rao garu"). He gave the markets/OI view and answered most of the viewer stock Q&A.

## Market view
- The **gap-down open is no big surprise** — global uncertainty persists, the road map is never decided, and Trump/Iran statements keep shaking markets on and off. A smooth journey from here is not possible.
- **Last week was positive**: benchmarks +1.5%; Nifty +~178 pts, Sensex +~414-420 pts, helped by rupee appreciation and strong domestic earnings. **Nifty Smallcap +4% (5th straight week up); Midcap +3.5% to an all-time high.**
- Larger players/HNIs are trading **stock-specific in the broader market**; frontline stocks ignored due to FII selling. **India unattractive to FIIs** vs other EMs (cited Samir Arora). Contrast: Kospi +78% YTD, Intel +250%, SanDisk +558% in 2026 — India ~9% in the minus.
- **Economy faces rough times over the next two quarters** — crude jump, rising fiscal/monetary deficit, inflation risk, possible diesel/petrol price hikes. OMCs may have to raise prices (monthly losses piling up).
- **Gold to moderate** on stalled US-Iran peace talks — mildly **positive for stocks**.
- Only support: **domestic institutions buying strongly.**

## Levels / OI
- **24,000 is the inflection point** — above it positive, below negative. Sideways range **23,600-24,500**, base **24,000**.
- Strong call OI at **24,200-24,500**; 24,200 strong resistance + selling last week.
- IV on calls/puts ~15-16.5%; **VIX below 17** (one day can change it). **PCR jumped to 1.28**, near peak ~1.4 — could fall again with selling.
- If oil jumps, indications of further Nifty losses.
