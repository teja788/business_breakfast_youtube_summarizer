## Top story — Telugu-states data-centre boom
- **Andhra Pradesh (Vizag)** is becoming the data-centre hub of India. A **Google data centre (1.5 GW)** with a **$15 bn** investment had its foundation-stone laid today.
- **Reliance Industries** is planning a larger data-centre cluster (**$19 bn**); the two together ~**$34 bn** (~Rs 3.5 lakh crore).
