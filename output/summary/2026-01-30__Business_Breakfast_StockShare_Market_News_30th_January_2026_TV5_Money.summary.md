## Headline
Friday before a **big-day Budget** (Budget session this week; markets work this Sunday and Monday, expiry Tuesday). Weak open expected — **GIFT Nifty ~150 points down** — driven by pre-Budget nervousness and a wild overnight commodities blow-up.

## Commodities — overnight "dhamaal"
- **Silver** spiked to **$121/oz** on COMEX, crashed to **$106**, now ~**$109-110**. In rupee terms it crossed **Rs 4,20,000/kg**, fell to **Rs 3,55,000** (down ~Rs 65,000 in hours), closed ~Rs 3,98,000-3,99,000.
- **Gold** crossed **$5,602/oz**, fell to $5,440, now ~$5,170-5,180 (**-4%**). MCX close **Rs 1,83,962**; likely to open ~Rs 1,77,000 (was near Rs 1,93,000 prior day).
- **Copper** +10% overnight to **$14,500**; a **commodity super-cycle** (copper, aluminium, zinc) is in play. FOMO chasing flagged as a risk.
- Note: **Tether (stablecoin)** reportedly bought **~140 tonnes of gold** over 14-15 months — a factor behind the gold rally.
