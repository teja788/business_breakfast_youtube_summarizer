- **Gold ~4,600** and **silver ~$83/oz** at record highs; commodities/metals being treated as **safe havens** amid global turmoil. Base metals (gold, silver, copper, zinc) being bought; even ferrous metals may follow.

## Indian markets
- **Nifty lost ~700 points over the week** (from Monday's high); another ~200-point fall Friday — five straight negative closings. Markets are **underperforming** global peers.
- **FPIs continuous sellers** — sold **Rs 3,769 cr** Friday; **DIIs bought Rs 5,600 cr**. FPI **short positions** rose from **1.28 lakh** to **1.86 lakh contracts** over the week (Nifty lot-size change a partial factor); Friday they unwound 1,000 longs and added 17,000 shorts. Only ~0.5% net long — a near-record short, **oversold** setup (**PCR 0.62**, was 0.66). A bounce is likely; an Oct-14-2025 analog saw Nifty rally ~900-1,000 points off similar extremes.
- **Pre-budget rally?** Under 15 sessions to the budget. A possible **FPI tax sweetener** (LTCG exemption) is a positive "feeler"; rupee depreciation and richer Indian valuations keep FPIs looking elsewhere.
- **Breadth very weak** — Friday A/D 577:2,384 (~1:5); **Midcap −2.6%**, **Smallcap −3.5%** (portfolios likely −5-6%). Nifty 500 breadth 87 advances vs 412 declines.
- **Levels:** 50-DMA broken; testing **100-DMA**; **200-DMA at 25,250**. Key support **25,500** must hold — break risks further weakness and a possible bear-market shift if earnings + budget disappoint. Currently a **"bear hug,"** not yet a bear market.
- **Earnings season** kicks off: **TCS** and **HCL Tech** Q3 today (watch AI commentary, IT spends); **Reliance** Friday; **ICICI Bank** and **HDFC Bank** Saturday — ~30-40% of Nifty weight reports this week.
- **Results so far:** **Tejas Networks** — terrible Q3 (**Rs 134 cr operating loss**, **Rs 197 cr net loss**; income YoY −88%); **DMart** weak initial updates but decent numbers (slight indirect drag on Reliance sentiment).
- **BSE in focus:** SEBI chief said no further derivatives fixes needed; **NSE IPO NOC** expected this month, IPO possibly next month.
- **Technicals (Ramakrishna):** Fibonacci retracements crossing 100% toward 160%; immediate Nifty support **25,560 / 25,500**; **RSI 38.55**, MACD negative crossover, at lower Bollinger band. **India VIX** rose 3% to **10.93** (off historic lows ~9.1-9.2), could rise to **13.5** by budget. Bank Nifty closed below 20-DMA and 50-DMA; supports far (~**58,750 / 58,000**, ~1,200 pts below), resistance **59,500 call / 25,800 Nifty call**. Intraday short idea: short below **25,590** (60-70 pt target, ~50 pt stop) if it holds 5 minutes.
- **Stance:** Capital protection; keep cash; no panic selling and no exuberant buying. Book partial profits where they exist; deploy any surplus slowly into good-earnings companies; keep mutual-fund SIPs running. Metal stocks (zinc, copper, Vedanta, Hindustan Copper, Hindalco, Hindustan Zinc) the lone relative bright spot.
