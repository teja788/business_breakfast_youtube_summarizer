## India–US trade deal (key event)
- Weekend **joint statement** received; net positive across sectors.
- **25% Russian-oil-linked tariffs removed**; **reciprocal tariffs cut from 25% to 18%** (some goods at **0%**).
- **Weighted-average US tariff on India to fall from 37.2% to 15.7%.** 18% reciprocal tariffs effective over the coming week.
- **0% on generic pharma / generic APIs and on electronics exports**; **agricultural products excluded** from the deal.
- Watch-points: US to closely monitor **India–Russia oil trade** (e.g. Reliance restarting imports after a 5-week pause); India's **$500bn purchase "intent"** (energy, aircraft) over 5 years is an intent, not a commitment.
