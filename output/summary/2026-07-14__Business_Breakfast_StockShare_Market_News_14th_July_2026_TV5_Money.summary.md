- **US closed red overnight** — **Nasdaq and S&P both ~-1%**. Asian cues negative in early trade; **GIFT Nifty lower**, pointing to a gap-down open.
- **Crude jumped back to $84 Brent** (almost **$85** after Trump's statement about a **20% levy tied to Hormuz [as heard]** because "America will protect it"); regular/WTI crude near **$80**. Kutumba Rao: once crude jumped, all global markets turned weakish — the geopolitical scenario must be watched carefully.
