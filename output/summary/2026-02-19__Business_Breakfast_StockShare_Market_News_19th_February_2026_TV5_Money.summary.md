## Global backdrop
- **GIFT Nifty pointed to a positive open**, but largely because **most Asian markets were shut** (only Kospi and Japan trading; the rest still in holiday mood).
- **US closed positive across the board** last night — **S&P 500, Nasdaq, Russell 2000 and Dow** all higher.
- The real story was **oil**: **crude jumped from $67 to near $70** on reports that **America is preparing to attack Iran** — possibly **this weekend**, since such moves usually happen over weekends. Market anxiety is about how far crude runs from here.
- Safe havens moved: **gold +2%**, **silver +5%** overnight on the Iran-America standoff.
- **US software stocks bounced back**: **EPAM +2.2%**, **Globant, Cognizant, Accenture +2-2.5%**, **Infosys ADR +2%**, Wipro ADR slightly up.
- **US Fed in no hurry to cut rates** → dollar gaining against other currencies; **rupee at 90.72** with no significant activity.
