- Mukesh Ambani also announced a **LEO satellite constellation** launch. Existing Jio backers: Reliance 66%, **Meta 10%, Google 7.73%**, PIF/KKR/Vista ~2.3-3% each.
- **Reliance + Jio combined** could reach ~**Rs 31 lakh crore**, lifting their share of India market cap from ~4% to ~7%.
- Also pending: **NSE IPO** (high PE), and next year **Carlyle**-linked healthcare names (RCM/Assets).
