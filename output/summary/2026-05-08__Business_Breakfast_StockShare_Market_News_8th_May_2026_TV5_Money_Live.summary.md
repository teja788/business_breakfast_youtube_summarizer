- **US markets**: Tech stocks performed well overnight, but the other two indices saw negative/loss reactions. Ongoing **rotation between AI/chip-manufacturing stocks and traditional software** — profit booking in chip names, recovery/buying in traditional software.
- **Geopolitics**: Fresh **US-Iran firing in the Persian Gulf**; both sides trading blame. Trump tweeted the ceasefire is still in effect, calling the latest exchange "just a **love tap**." Markets treat it as a small skirmish, not escalation.
- **Crude**: **Brent ~101-102**; dipped to ~98 intraday before ticking back up on tensions. Analysts note a further **~10-dollar fall (toward 80-90)** would be a positive trigger for Indian markets.
