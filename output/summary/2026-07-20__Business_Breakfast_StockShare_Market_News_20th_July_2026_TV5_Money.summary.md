# Summary — Business Breakfast, 20 July 2026 (TV5 Money)
# https://youtu.be/WDKV58bLen4

## Global backdrop
- **West Asia crisis entered "Phase 2" over the weekend.** US Central Command said it struck **Iranian highways and bridges**; Iran said it **blew up two oil tankers transiting the Strait of Hormuz**. **Brent crude jumped to $90** — unthinkable 15-20 days ago when 85-86 looked like the ceiling. **$100 can't be ruled out**; the geopolitical environment "changed completely in the last 48 hours."
- **Crude at $90 is the single biggest negative** for Indian equities and the economy. On the crude charts, Kranthi flagged an indication that crude **may go above $100/bbl**.
- **The AI rally is fizzling out.** **Philadelphia Semiconductor Index -20% from its June high**; **Nasdaq -4% last week, -7% from its high, now below its 50-day moving average**; selling in AMD, Intel and the chip complex. Concerns: cost of computing must fall, and whether the **hundreds of billions of dollars of capex** will really earn a return.
- **Korea -10% last week** (still **+83% YTD**), **Taiwan -6% last week**. **TSMC announced a $270 bn US investment** (fabs in America, plus Japan). India's **Semicon Mission 1.0 + 2.0 combined is under $25-30 bn** — "we are still a very marginal player." Bright spot: **Seoul Semiconductor** is scouting **Tamil Nadu** for a chip plant under **ISM 2.0**.
- GIFT Nifty pointed to a **negative opening**; Asian markets in a downtrend.

## Indian markets
- **Blockbuster weekend earnings.** **Reliance Industries crossed Rs 3 lakh crore of quarterly revenue for the first time**, with a full **O2C turnaround** after 5-6 laggard quarters; only blemish was **Jio Platforms PAT down ~2.16% sequentially** (up YoY). **Goldman Sachs' Rs 1,870** is the highest street target.
- **ICICI Bank was the star** — "super-duper," **RoA 2.49%**, beating on slippages, margins, fee income and NIMs; Sandeep Bakshi now "the best banker." **HDFC Bank failed** — **profit +5% only**, **NIM 3.26% (a record low)** and a visible **leadership vacuum** (board differences, Chakravarty's exit, operational allegations).
- Other banks: **Kotak decent (PAT +26% YoY**, provisions down, but **CEO uncertainty)**; **Axis so-so**; **RBL weak** (slippages up, NIMs down); **Federal Bank excellent**; **PNB magnificent**; **Punjab & Sind Bank good**.
- **Q1 is a beat**: **net sales growth at a 14-quarter high**, **fastest earnings growth in 11 quarters**, **67% of results positive so far**. Headlines: "Early birds set a strong Q1 tone." **~250-260 companies report this week**. Even IT showed no negatives — **Infosys numbers due this week**, management commentaries positive, **Tech Mahindra strong on Friday**.
- **Friday's session**: Nifty **+260 points on the day** (**+127 on the week**), **Bank Nifty +939** — banks and IT pulled together — but **broader markets underperformed** with mid/small-cap selling.
- **Monday's open reversed it**: pre-open Nifty **-144**, opened **-85**; **Bank Nifty -600/-700 (later -760)**, **Sensex -470**. **ICICI Bank +1.3%** couldn't carry it alone against **HDFC Bank -4.5%, Axis -4.5%, Kotak -3%**. Advance-decline ~**1:1** with declines a shade higher; oil & gas and energy the only positive sectors. **Rupee opened weak at 96.40**.
- **Movers**: **Natco Pharma +4%** on a US FDA product approval; gains in PNB, Aegis Logistics, Neuland, Nalco, GE Shipping, Inox India, Kirloskar Oil Engines, Godrej Industries, Solar Industries, Tata Communications, Craftsman, Metropolis. Losers: **India Cements -5.5%** (results disappointed), **Yes Bank -3%**, Premier Energies, eClerx, Reliance Infra, Campus Activewear, Clean Science, ITC Hotels, Waaree, Transformers & Rectifiers.
- **Technicals (Ramakrishna)**: the market **broke out of the four-week symmetrical triangle and closed outside**, but hurdles remain at **24,530 / 24,550 / 24,576** (April-May and July highs). **Bank Nifty resistance 58,700**. OI: **24,700 calls** heaviest writing, **24,200** on the put side; Bank Nifty **59,000 calls / 58,000 puts**. **PCR 1.07**, **India VIX ~13** — comfortable, no fear yet. **DIIs still net buyers.** Momentum weak from the long consolidation: **RSI ~60, below the MACD signal line, red histogram bars**. Intraday: **long only above 24,262**, else a **five-minute short below 24,180**.
- **Telugu-pride story**: **Skyroot** (Hyderabad) flew **Vikram-1 on a maiden orbital mission** — the first private Indian rocket to space outside ISRO. **Solar Industries' Rs 18 crore 2021 investment is now worth Rs 950 crore**; **Inox India** and **Diffusion Engineers** are also associated. Skyroot targets **one launch a month**.
- **Results scoreboard read out**: good numbers from **Bhansali Engineering Polymers (BEPL), Tatva Chintan, Poonawalla Fincorp, Globus Spirits, Tata Technologies, Steel Strips & Wheels, SG Finserve, Premier Polyfilm, Navkar, Menon Bearings, Polycab, BHEL, GNA Axles, Piramal Finance, Borosil Renewables, Jio Finance, Fedfina, Groww, Jana SFB, Himadri Speciality, MRPL, Emmvee Photovoltaic, Bajaj Consumer, JSW Steel, ITC Hotels, HDB Financial Services, Angel One**. **Kalyan Jewellers rose ~50-60% last week** off its lows.
