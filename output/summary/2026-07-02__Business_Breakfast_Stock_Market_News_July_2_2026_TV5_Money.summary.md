## Global backdrop
- Global cues mixed/slightly negative but **GIFT Nifty positive**, indicating a **90-100 point gap-up** open.
- US overnight: **Nasdaq -0.66%, S&P 500 -0.22%, Dow flat**. Kospi down 3-4%, other Asian markets soft, MSCI EM index closed lower — while India closed up, signalling **India outperformance**.
- **Meta +9%** on entering the cloud-infrastructure business (competing with Azure/AWS). Traditional software companies (e.g., Palantir peers) rallied on a growing "anti-AI-tokenization" narrative — criticism that AI firms (Anthropic/Claude, Grok, etc.) are monetizing wrongly and should be open-source; sparking talk of a possible **AI-trade reversal** and cost-benefit/ROI doubts on AI capex.
- US **Q2 earnings season** starting; market expects **18-22% earnings growth**.
- **Crude oil** decisively below **$70/barrel** (down from a war-driven $120 peak) — a major positive for India; West Asia crisis largely defused.

## Indian markets
- **Nifty closed +140 pts above 24,000; Sensex +450 pts** yesterday — first signs of the anticipated **H2-CY26 India outperformance**.
- **Sector rotation**: previously unloved/oversold **FMCG** (HUL, Asian Paints, ITC, Nestle, Dabur), **Realty** (Lodha, DLF, Godrej Properties) and platform names (Paytm, Lenskart, Eternal) rallied hard; market breadth positive. FMCG buying linked to strong **GST data** (implies economic/employment strength) and hopes of monsoon-led rural recovery.
- **IT/Pharma lagged** as domestic-facing sectors led; **KPIT -15%** yesterday. Anchor reiterated a standing "avoid IT" call — business-model risk, likely profit warnings ahead, no bottom-fishing on cheapness alone.
- **FIIs**: light net sellers (~Rs 1,140 cr) but sitting on **~90% short positions** in F&O entering July — a potential **short-covering trigger** if the "feel-good factor" strengthens. **DIIs bought Rs 3,159 cr.**
- **Nifty levels**: resistance **24,250-24,400** (per Kranthi) / **24,260-300** (per Ramakrishna); support **23,800** (important zone since June 15). Bank Nifty: resistance **~58,700**, support **~57,000**; RSI 63, above all MAs incl. 200-DMA.
- **PCR 1.13**, India VIX cooled to ~2.63(as stated). Highest OI: **24,000 calls**; Bank Nifty **58,300 calls / 58,000 puts**.
- **Macro positives**: GST collections **+14% YoY**, car sales **+24% YoY** avg, strong UPI transactions and freight loading — signalling economy resilience despite global "turbulence" (Trump-related trade noise).
- Market called a **"buy on dips"** market; stock selection should focus on **earnings visibility + management commentary/execution track record**, not just cheapness.
- **Market open**: Nifty +80, Sensex +230, Bank Nifty +110-120; breadth positive (NSE 1,446 adv/372 dec). Mid-cap standouts: **SCTL** (upper circuit again, band cut 10%→5%), **Sai Parentals** (+8% on a Rs 1,300-cr Australian "Nawmed" contract renewal, ~80-90% above its Rs 392 IPO price), **GNG Electronics** (near upper circuit, laptop-refurbishment play, new Redington tie-up). Losers: **Zee Entertainment** (-3%, promoter warrant issue), **HFCL** (-3%, profit booking), KPR Mills, Aegis Logistics, Apar Industries, Sai Life Sciences, RITES, GE T&D, Oil India.
