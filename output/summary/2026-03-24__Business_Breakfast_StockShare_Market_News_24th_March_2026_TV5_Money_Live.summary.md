## Global backdrop
- **Trump flip-flop on Iran:** After a 48-hour ultimatum, **Donald Trump** reversed course within 24 hours and announced a **five-day ceasefire/pause**, saying no attacks on Iran's energy infrastructure and that talks are "making good progress" (posted on Truth Social).
- **Iran pushback:** Iran's Foreign Minister called it **fake news**, alleging Trump is **manipulating oil and financial markets**; the Iranian Parliament Speaker also denied any talks. **Netanyahu** said Israel is "trying to stop the war."
- **Pre-open anomaly:** Around **6:50am US time** (before the 7:00 statement at ~7:04am), billions of dollars of shares were bought in **S&P 500 pre-open** — raising **insider-trading suspicions**.
- **Market moves:** S&P 500 futures **+2.5% to +5%**; **NYMEX crude -10%** (from ~USD 98 to USD 86, below USD 90); **Brent** from ~USD 113-114 to below USD 100, now ~USD 104. **Dow** gained as much as **1,100 points**, closed **+600**.
- **Asia mixed:** Hang Seng **+1.3%**, Nikkei **+0.7%**; **Dow futures ~250 points negative** by morning. Anchor coined the **"TACO" (Trump Always Chickens Out)** trade.
- **Key signal to watch:** normal vessel/cargo traffic resuming through the **Strait of Hormuz** = real end of conflict.

## Indian markets
- **GIFT Nifty** had been up **800-900 points** overnight, cooled to **~300-360 points** gap-up by morning.
- **Wealth destruction:** **Rs 15 lakh crore** of investor wealth lost in a single day yesterday; **Rs 50 lakh crore** eroded over the last 24 days (since Feb 28) on BSE market cap.
- **Yesterday's flows:** **FIIs sold > Rs 10,000 crore**, **DIIs bought > Rs 12,000 crore**; Nifty closed **600 points negative** (below 22,500) after a late recovery during the PM's Parliament speech.
- **Open (pre-open trade):** Nifty **+370 points**, Bank Nifty **+947 points**; cash open **~22,815 (+~300)**. Advance/decline strongly positive (BSE 2,034 adv / 315 dec; NSE 2,148 adv / 127 dec) — reverse of yesterday's close.
- **Volatility:** **India VIX spiked to 26.73** (highest in nearly two years); FII **put-call ratio 0.84**. **Rupee** near **93.94-94/USD**.
- **Gold:** fell to ~**USD 4,200**, recovered to **~USD 4,350** with Trump statement and rising crude; analyst doubts precious metals will keep acting as a safe haven.
- **Sector/stock action at open:** gains in consumer durables, auto, metals, PSU & private banks, oil & gas; **Asian Paints +3%** (price-hike news); buying in **Electra Greentech, JBM Auto, GR Infra** (order-win). **HDFC Bank down ~4%** despite the rally (bank-specific concerns not being digested).
- Two IPOs opening today: **Pavarika** and **Sai Parenteral**.

## Sai Parenteral IPO (featured)
- **Hyderabad-based injectables/CDMO maker**, promoted by MD **K. Anil Kumar**. **Raise Rs 409 crore**, price band **Rs 372-392**, **listing April 2**.
- **Valuation:** FY25 EPS **Rs 5.43**, **PE ~72x** (anchor called it ~70x), P/Rev **6.4x**, P/B **10.9x** — "expensive" on trailing, but anchor's forward case sees **EBITDA ~Rs 305 crore by FY28-30 (50% CAGR)**, **fair value ~Rs 700/share (~78% upside by FY30)**.
- **Anchor book ~Rs 146 crore** (oversubscribed vs Anil's Rs 122 crore portion); names include **Kotak (incl. 100% pharma fund), Morgan Stanley, India Emerging Fund (Dubai), Blue Lotus Fund**.
- **Story:** acquired **74.6% of Australian Newmed Pharmaceuticals** (451 IP dossiers) for Rs 131 crore; backward integration in Australia with a **non-repayable USD 20m grant** (40% of USD 51m plant cost), targeting **~3x margin** uplift. Pre-IPO/early backers: **Mohandas Pai (Aarin Capital), Nikhil Kamath (Gruhas), Polycab family office, KIMS' Bhaskar Rao**. Promoter stake 61% pre → ~52% post.
