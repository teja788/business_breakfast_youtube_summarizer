## Global backdrop
- **US markets closed mixed** overnight: **Dow** slightly lower, **Nasdaq** and **S&P 500** positive. November **jobless claims** came in lower than expected.
- Market is nearly certain of a **Fed rate cut at the December 10 meeting**; US futures green.
- **Asian markets mixed.** Gold, silver and crude all **range-bound**.
- **Marc Faber** flagged that the coming year is "**not a great year for equities, especially India**."

## Indian markets — the day's driver
- All eyes on the **RBI monetary policy at 10 a.m.** Expectation of a **0.25% (25 bps) rate cut**, but very strong **Q2 GDP of 8.2%** and **Fitch's GDP upgrade to 7.4%** muddy the odds. Whether the **RBI revises its GDP forecast** and its **rupee/inflation commentary** are seen as more crucial than the cut itself. Inflation described as **benign/soft**, with some talk of negative inflation.
- **FII selling persists**: sold **Rs 1,944 cr** yesterday vs **DII buying of Rs 3,641 cr**; ~**Rs 15,000 cr** FII selling over the last six days (double all of November). **Rupee weak, above 90**; RBI not defending it (REER-realistic ~94.75).
- **Nifty levels:** support **25,900–26,000**, resistance **26,100** and the all-time high **26,325**. Sustaining **26,000** is the key; call OI heavy at **26,500**. **PCR 0.93** (below 1 but improving); **VIX** dropped ~4% to **10.82** (below 11).
- **Bank Nifty:** doji/indecision formation; OI concentrated at **59,500**; consolidation-to-positive bias post-policy. Pre-open indicated a **30–35 pt Nifty loss**; markets opened roughly **flat** (Sensex -56 pts).
- **Events/flows:** **Putin in Delhi** (defence deals — HAL, BDL firm); **BAT selling 7–15% of ITC Hotels** (~Rs 3,000 cr, around Rs 205); **Meesho (8.99x)** and **Aequs (~30x)** IPOs close today; **Kwality Wall's** lists; **ICICI Prudential AMC** IPO tomorrow. Heavy IPO/block-deal pipeline is draining **market liquidity**.
- **IndiGo** operational meltdown (mass cancellations) — seen as a **big negative**, possible 14–15% correction.
