## Global backdrop
- **Gift Nifty** pointed to a mildly positive open (~**25,436**, +10 pts). Asia broadly higher; **South Korea** firm despite a **25% US tariff** order.
- **S&P 500** touched a fresh lifetime high (**+0.41%**); **Nasdaq +~0.9%**, led by tech.
- **Dollar index** very weak, down near **96** overnight. Trump dismissive ("if the dollar falls, what's the loss to me… the dollar is doing great").
- Dollar weakness drove **precious metals**: **Gold** holding above **$5,200**; **Silver** ~**$1,154**, up ~**8%** overnight. Gold/silver ETFs "running at lightning speed."
- **Rupee** recovered slightly to **91.92** (prev close ~91.7271) but flagged as among the weakest of the world's major currencies — negative for crude/gold imports, foreign travel, overseas education.
- Retail **gold sales reportedly down 70%** at ~**Rs 1.6 lakh/tola**.

## Indian markets
- **Budget on Sunday, 1 February** (Nirmala Sitharaman) is the next big event; budget sessions begin today. Hope/expectations crowded onto the budget now that the **India–EU FTA** is concluded.
- **Pre-budget weakness historic:** Sensex **-3.9%** over the last month — weakest pre-budget run since **2016**. **Small-cap index -~7%** over the month.
- Yesterday's **expiry session very volatile**; Nifty closed **+~120 pts** (~25,175) on **short covering**. **Bank Nifty +732 pts** to ~**59,200** — clear January-series winner.
- **PSU re-rating milestone:** **SBI overtook ICICI Bank** to become the **2nd-largest bank by market cap** (after HDFC Bank). Ranking: HDFC, SBI, ICICI, **Axis** (now 4th, above Kotak), Kotak. Axis up **4%** on better-than-Kotak numbers.
- **Mid/small-cap pain:** despite good results, no buying. F&O losers over the month: **Syngene -24.8%, Godrej Properties -24.3%, Kalyan Jewellers -24%, ITC -20%, HPCL/Dixon -15%**; plus Swiggy, NBCC, CG Power, Tube Investments, Premier Energies. Sentiment-driven, not fundamentals.
- **Bondada Engineering** (Hyderabad solar EPC) posted strong Q3 — **net profit +119% YoY to Rs 54 cr**, **revenue +89% to Rs 712 cr**, **EBITDA +121% (~Rs 85 cr)**, margins **10.2%→11.9%** — yet stock near 52-week low (~320 vs 530 high) and **fell ~4%** on the day.
- **FIIs net sellers ~Rs 1,952 cr**; **DIIs rescued the market** (~Rs 7,760–9,000 cr buying). Bull rally needs FIIs to stop selling.
- **Options data:** highest call OI **25,500**, put **25,000** → expected **500-pt range (25,000–25,500)** into the budget; **26,000** the upside target if budget pleases. **India VIX** spiked to **16.06** intraday, settled ~**14.45**. Next expiry **3 February** (post-budget).
- **OFS:** **Vedanta** offering ~**1.6% of Hindustan Zinc** (~**Rs 4,600 cr**) at a **6% discount**; HZ -2–3%, Vedanta +2%.
- **Results today:** L&T, Maruti Suzuki, Cochin Shipyard, GRSE, TVS Motors, Lodha, Phoenix, Piramal Pharma, SBI Cards. **Asian Paints -6%** on a miss; **IGI +9%**, **Mahindra Logistics**, **Bikaji/Gopal snacks** strong.
