## Global backdrop
- US markets closed higher again; **Nasdaq and S&P 500 near record highs**, AI trade continuing. Selling in traditional IT (e.g. **EPAM**), buying in AI names like **Nvidia**.
- **Marvell Technologies jumped 32%** after Nvidia's Jensen Huang called it the "next trillion-dollar company." Note: Marvell has a significant India presence (Hyderabad).
