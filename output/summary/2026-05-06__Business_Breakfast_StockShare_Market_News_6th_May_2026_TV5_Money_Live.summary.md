## Global backdrop
- **GIFT Nifty** indicated a **gap-up open of ~150 points**; a "sandwich session" between two expiries.
- **Iran/US**: Trump's "**Project Freedom**" (post-Operation Epic Fury) passed to ease vessel transit through the **Strait of Hormuz**, but the blockade on Iranian vessels continues; **ceasefire to continue**. Seen as a positive de-escalation; war seen as "almost over."
- **US tech roaring**: Nasdaq/S&P 500 at **all-time highs**. **Micron** crossed **$700bn** mkt cap; **SK Hynix** crossed **$1 trillion**. Trillion-dollar club: Nvidia ~$5T, Alphabet ~$4T, Apple $3.8T, Microsoft $2.8T, plus Amazon, TSMC, Broadcom, Meta, Tesla, Berkshire.
- **AI/semiconductor revolution**: leaders growing **35-40% annually** even on huge bases; **Intel up ~13%** (multibagger since US govt stake). Apple investing in Intel; inviting Intel/SK Hynix to make device processors in the US.
- **Cloud backlogs huge**: Google Cloud **$460bn**, AWS **$364bn** Q1 backlog; **Anthropic-Google ~$200bn / 5-yr deal**. Crude cooled to **~$108** (from $112-113).

## Indian markets
- **Govt stimulus**: Cabinet approved a **Rs 2.55 lakh crore** credit lifeline — **100% credit guarantee for MSMEs**, **90% five-year loans** for non-MSMEs/airlines, **1-year moratorium** (Rs 100 cr for small cos, Rs 1,500 cr for airlines). Drove **banking stocks** and **IndiGo/InterGlobe**.
- **Reform**: **Google** got India's **first private discom (distribution) license** for its Andhra Pradesh data center — a notable reform.
- **Nifty levels**: closed above **24,000** (positive); **24,500** = major resistance, **24,000** = key benchmark/support. PCR jumped to **1.08**. Opened +200; **Bank Nifty +700 (~1.2-2%)**. Advance/decline ~**7:1 to 8:1**. FIIs **+Rs 3,600 cr**, DIIs **+Rs 2,600 cr**.
- **Smallcap momentum**: ET banner story — smallcap-100 **+18.4% in April**; momentum favoring small/mid caps (historical 15%+ months: 2020, 2014 +22.4%, May 2009 +47%).

## Results season (broadly strong)
- **Blockbuster**: **Lloyds Metals & Energy** — record revenue **Rs 631 cr** (5x YoY), PAT **Rs 1,530 cr** (~7-8x YoY, +50% QoQ), cash flow Rs 920 cr.
- **Good**: Hero MotoCorp, M&M (SUV king), Poonawalla Fincorp (rev Rs 215 cr, ~2x; PAT Rs 255 cr Q4), Coforge (EBIT/margins up, +8%), Aadhar Housing Fin, Raymond Realty, Aeroflex, JNK Electronics, SJS Enterprises.
- **Disappointing**: **L&T** — margins/EBIT weak (stock -3%), though order book very strong at **Rs 7.4 lakh cr / $80bn** (beat FY26 guidance). **TCS** down ~10-15% post-results — IT called "wealth destroyers."
