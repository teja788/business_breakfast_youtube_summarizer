## Indian markets
- Yesterday (Jan 1) India was the only major market trading; thin, holiday-mood volumes. **Nifty** closed up **~17 pts** in a narrow ~80-84 pt range (high 26,113/26,197). **Bank Nifty +30 pts**; Sensex slightly negative. **Nifty Next 50** and **Midcap 100** outperformed.
- **ITC** was the biggest drag — about **95 Nifty points** alone — on a coming "sin tax." A **National Calamity Contingent Duty (NCCD)** + central excise hike on tobacco takes effect **Feb 1** (GST route not yet ready). **Godfrey Phillips -17%, ITC -9%**; NTC, VST also fell. Cigarette prices could rise up to **15%**, with some volume hit. ITC saw **Rs 6,000 cr** delivery-based selling.
- **Flows:** FIIs net sellers again at **Rs 3,268 cr**; DIIs bought only **Rs 1,525 cr**.
- **Macro:** December **GST revenue Rs 1,76,857 cr**, YoY growth just **1.3%** (vs Rs 1,70,276 cr last Nov) — muted, weak festive sales.
- **Corporate actions / news:**
  - **QSR consolidation:** **Sapphire Foods** to merge into **Devyani International** — swap **1.77:1** (better than the 1:2 feared), so Devyani **+7%**, Sapphire **-6%** intraday. Consolidates KFC/Pizza Hut brands.
  - **Indian Bank** Q update strong (total business **+13.2%**, advances **+14.2%**); **Punjab & Sind Bank** weak.
  - Government awarded a fresh **Rs 20,000 cr** highway order (Solapur–Nashik, Maharashtra).
  - **MCX** ex-split today (trading ~2,227 post-split).
  - Big 2026 IPO pipeline cited: **BOAT, NSE, OYO, PhonePe, Reliance Jio, SBI Mutual Fund, Zepto** and more.
- **Q1 outlook:** Earnings season, Budget, and a possible trade deal make the quarter eventful; results begin in ~5-6 sessions.
- **Open day:** Nifty opened **+27-28 pts**, Sensex **+110**, Bank Nifty **+135**; autos led on strong monthly sales; **ITC down a further ~4%**.
