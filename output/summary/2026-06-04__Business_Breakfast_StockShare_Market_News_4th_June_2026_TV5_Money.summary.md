## Indian markets
- **Gap-down opening** expected. Yesterday Nifty lost **76-77 points** but **Bank Nifty closed up ~470 points** (outperformer), with IT dragging the index.
- IT pack in a **bear market**: **TCS -7.5%** yesterday; 2300 call premium fell 72% while put premium rose 600%. Advice: avoid large-cap IT; selectively buy mid/small-cap IT (Coforge, Oracle Financial, Persistent).
- **Nifty technicals**: touched **23,150** (filled the 8 April gap); support **23,000-150 / 23,270**, resistance **23,800**. Lower lows forming (23,922 on 25 May → 23,151 on 3 June), but mid/small-caps stay resilient despite FII selling.
- **RBI monetary policy tomorrow** is the key trigger (repo rate, yields, rupee commentary). RBI **rejected treasury-bill bids** (weak demand); **rupee weakened to ~95.71** (near 96).
- News flow: government proposal to **mix petrol into ethanol** (positive for sugar/ethanol makers); rumours of **STCG/LTCG relief for FPIs** and a cut to the **20% tax on overseas bond-investor interest** (Bloomberg-reported), which helped bonds recover — "buy on rumours, sell on news."
- **Rajesh Exports**: SEBI interim order alleges **Rs 15 lakh crore of fictitious transactions (2021-25)**; repeatedly flagged as untrustworthy. Stock rose to Rs 109 (+2.2%) but likely heading to lower circuit. LIC and two FIIs each hold >10%.
- Cabinet decisions: **Rs 10,000 crore Aviation/ATF relief fund**; Delhi old-vehicle replacement (positive for electric buses); Telangana highway expansion.
- Bloomberg "RBI sold ~12 billion dollars of gold" story called false; **RBI issued a rejoinder** (reserves ~880 tonnes "as on date"); Kutumba Rao noted the undated wording fuels confusion.
- Stock-specific moves at open: **Trent** ex-bonus; **JBM Auto +5%** (claims 49% e-bus market share, 157 buses delivered last month); **Olectra +2.5%** (Rs 500 crore expansion); IdeaForge and Skipper announced QIP/preferential issues; Savita Oil and Akshar Chem +20% on results.
- At open: **Nifty ~-130**, **Sensex ~-450**, **Bank Nifty -200+** (underperforming early); **VIX +6% to 16.28**.
