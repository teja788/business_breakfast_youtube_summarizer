## Global backdrop
- **Nasdaq** closed slightly positive after 2-3 down sessions as tech selling eased (**Broadcom, Nvidia, Oracle**). Asian screens positive, but **Gift Nifty** signalled a negative open.
- **Brent crude** below $60, ~**$59.5** (touched $58.85); **WTI** below **$55**, lowest since **Feb 2021** — driven by oversupply and Russia-Ukraine peace talks. Venezuela (US "foreign terrorist" designation) added a small ~1% bounce.
- **Gold** up ~**3%** overnight; **silver** very strong, above **$65** internationally.

## Currency & flows
- **Rupee** crossed **91/USD** (took only 10 days to slide from 90 to 91); down ~**6%** this year vs Indonesian rupiah -3.5%, Malaysian ringgit +9.5%, Thai baht +8%. RBI seen letting it weaken (dollar index only 98.27). Analysts warn 92-93 possible, even 100 over 1-2 years.
- **FIIs sold Rs 2,382 cr** yesterday; **DIIs bought only Rs 1,077 cr** — first net-institutional-selling since **Oct 30** (DIIs had bought 77 straight days). FII **net longs only 9%** (91% short).
- Watch factors: delayed **Indo-US trade deal**, possible **yen carry trade** unwind, AI-bubble "safe haven" narrative for India.

## Indian markets
- **NSE turnover** at a **two-year low (~Rs 90,000 cr)**, lowest since Nov 2023 — sharp lack of interest.
- Sharp divergence: **large caps** held up in 2025 (Maruti, Eicher, SBI; Reliance +17-18%/yr) while **mid/small/micro caps** in deep pain (many down 50-60%; defence stocks at 52-wk lows or -50-60%). Solar under a cloud (slow tendering, PPAs unsigned); railways/defence weak.
- Liquidity migrating from old stocks to **new IPOs/recent listings**; MFs shifting into RTAs. Nifty opened flat (~25,863), traded ~23 pts positive; Bank Nifty -57 pts.
- **Ola Electric** promoter sold ~2% (Rs 260 cr) near ATL to clear a loan / release pledged shares (still holds 34%); stock fell ~8%.
- **Block deals/OFS**: AkzoNobel Rs 1,200 cr block at 13% discount (opened ~-14-15%); Indian Overseas Bank ~3% govt OFS near Rs 34; Steel Exchange India promoters pledging 1.45 cr shares.
- **IPO frenzy**: ICICI Prudential AMC IPO subscribed **39x** (>Rs 10,000 cr issue; ~Rs 3 lakh cr bids); 2nd largest after LG Electronics (11,600 cr, 54x). Healthcare pipeline ~Rs 20,000 cr (Yashoda confidential filing, Gujarat Kidney, Star Hospitals ahead). Today's listings: Nephrocare (14x subscribed) and Park Medi World.
- Gas reform: govt announced **"one nation, one price, one grid"** — positive for city-gas (Mahanagar Gas, IGL). Vedanta got **NCLT approval** for demerger.
