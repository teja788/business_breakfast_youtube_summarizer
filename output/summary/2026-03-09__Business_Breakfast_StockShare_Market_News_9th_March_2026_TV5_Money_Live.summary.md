## Global backdrop
- A worrying Monday: **Dow Futures down ~1,100 points**, **Gift Nifty pointing to ~800-point loss**, equity markets turbulent worldwide.
- **Brent crude up ~28% overnight, Nymex ~30%, now near 118 dollars** (vs ~90 on Friday). Anchor frames it as heading toward **150 dollars**; **dollar index near a 3-month high**.
- Driver: the **Iran war / Strait of Hormuz** standoff — ~**20 million barrels/day** of crude supply effectively halted; **UAE, Kuwait, Qatar curbing production** for lack of storage. US reportedly hit an Iranian water-desalination plant; Iran reportedly hit a Bahraini one.
- The show argues the conflict is now a **prolonged war (3-6+ months)** between two strong states, not a short skirmish; **Iran's late Khamenei succeeded by his son**, ~80% domestic backing claimed.
- **Japan -7%, China -3%+, US futures -1,000+ pts; gold ~-2% to ~5050 dollars/oz**. Risk of world recession flagged by some economists.
- **Qatar invoked force majeure** on gas contracts; **GNFC halted production** for want of LNG.

## Impact on India
- India imports **~5 million barrels/day (~200 million dollars/day, ~70 billion dollars/year)**; oil bill could **cross 100 billion dollars**.
- Rule of thumb cited: **every 10 dollars on crude ≈ ~0.3% on CAD and ~1% GDP-growth hit**; crude up ~60 dollars implies **~3% GDP impact**. Rupee, forex reserves, and stocks all pressured.
- India holds only **~25-35 days of oil reserves** (vs China's ~3 months); Russian oil is the cushion.
- **Indo-US trade deal pushed out 3-4 months** (US Supreme Court tariff judgment, refunds/revisions); March deadline effectively shelved.

## Strategy themes
- **Be defensive, stock-specific, strict stop-loss, protect gains; cash is king.** Avoid catching the falling knife; **sell on rallies / dead-cat bounces** (the earlier "buy on dips" stance is over).
- **Pockets of safety: pharma** (Natco, Laurus named), **essential-commodity manufacturers**, **defence**, **upstream oil**, and partly **sugar** (oil-proxy).
- **Avoid pressure sectors** when oil rises: aviation, paints, chemicals, logistics; also fertilisers (gas-dependent), ceramics, autos, EMS at risk on supply-chain disruption.
- **Hedging tip (Kutumba Rao):** hedge the portfolio with **Nifty puts** (illustration: ~1 crore portfolio → ~26,000 put / ~8 lots, held till the market bottoms), or stock-level options if experienced; don't hedge "for the sake of it."
