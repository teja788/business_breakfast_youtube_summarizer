## Global backdrop
- **Last three trading sessions of 2025.** GIFT Nifty pointed to a mildly positive open (~30 points). Asian markets (Hang Seng, Taiwan, Korea) mostly in the green; US closed higher Friday with the **S&P 500 just 71 points from the 7000** milestone.
- **The year of AI and metals.** AI stocks exploded globally; **Nvidia became the first company ever to hit a 5 trillion dollar market cap.** Record rally in precious metals — **silver +170% (best in 46 years), gold +71.5% (strongest annual gain since 1917).** Silver's market cap crossed **4 trillion dollars**, now the 3rd-largest asset class after gold and Nvidia, bigger than Apple. Platinum, palladium near record highs; **copper hit 12,950 dollars** for the first time.
- **2025 index scorecard:** Nasdaq **+22.4%**, S&P 500 **+18.1%**, Dow **+14.9%**; KOSPI **+72%** (top Asian gainer), Hang Seng **+32%**, Nikkei **+29%** (all-time record), Shanghai **+21.5%**, FTSE **+19.5%**, DAX **+21.6%**.

## Indian markets
- **A year of underperformance.** Nifty rose only **~9.5%**, Midcap **+5%**, and Smallcap **down 6.7%**. Most retail portfolios are in the red vs a year ago.
- **Main cause: FPI selling.** Foreign outflows: **Dec ~24,000 cr, Nov 17,500 cr, Jul ~48,000 cr, Aug 47,000 cr, Sep 35,000 cr.** Whether they turn net buyers in 2026 hinges on the earnings season and Budget.
- **Retail SIPs held the market up.** 2025 SIP inflows hit **3 lakh crore** (~25-27k cr/month); flexi-cap, ETF, gold-ETF and multi-asset funds saw the strongest flows.
- **Near-term calendar:** IIP data due today; **monthly Nifty options expiry tomorrow (30 Dec)**; global markets half-day on 31 Dec (Indian market full day). Consolidation / holiday mood expected to persist.
- **Stock-specific news:** **Coforge** to acquire US AI firm **Encora for ~2.35 bn dollars (Rs 17,000 cr, all-stock)**, issuing 9.4 cr shares at 1816 (14.5% premium); EPS-dilutive near term, hold. **Defence Acquisition Council** meeting today may clear **~80,000 cr** of deals — defence stocks in focus. **Timex** OFS (9% promoter stake, floor 275) — opened down 9%. **Sigachi** down 4% after promoter remand over the recent fire.
