## Global backdrop
- **US–Iran talks failed** over the weekend (negotiations led by JD Vance and the Iranian Parliament Speaker). The two-week ceasefire still holds, but Trump again threatened to "block" the Strait of Hormuz (already effectively shut). Markets fell across Asia.
- **Crude spiked ~9%:** crude crossed $104, Brent above $102 (vs $95–96 over the weekend). Markets now move directly with crude. S&P 500 futures -0.75%, Dow futures ~-360.
